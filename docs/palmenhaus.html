Palmenhaus Flughafen Tempelhof

Illustration & Technical Drawing | 2017

Palmenhaus is a temporary structure designed to serve as an information and education hub for refugees living in the Flughafen Tempelhof refugee camp. The site is integrated with the InfoCompass platform to connect users with local social initiatives, providing vital information and guidance to people looking to establish their life in Berlin.
 
Palmenhaus was conceptualized and designed as a collaborative venture between place/making and Atelier Fanelsa.
